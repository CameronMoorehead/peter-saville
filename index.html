PETER SAVILLE

Peter Saville is an English art director and graphic designer. He came to fame for the many record sleeves he desiged for Factory Records, of which he was a director.

Born in Manchester, Lancashire, Saville attended St Ambrose College where studied graphic design at Manchester Polytechnic from 1975 to 1978. Saville entered the music scene soon after, evenutally becoming a partner at Factory Records along with Tony Wilson, Martin Hannett, Rob Gretton, and Alan Erasmus.

Peter Saville's most notable work on record sleeves was done for Joy Division and later New Order, but other artists include David Byrne, Brian Eno, Orchestral Manouveres in the Dark (OMD), Ultravox, King Crimson, and Peter Gabriel.

This page is dedicated to the wonderful works of art that are featured on so many iconic record sleeves of the 1980's and beyond
